About Upper Bicutan National High School

A Commitment to Academic Excellence and Holistic Growth

Upper Bicutan National High School (UBNHS) is dedicated to providing quality education that fosters both academic success and the personal development of every student. Our aim is to create a learning environment where students can grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially, preparing them for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

At UBNHS, our mission is to provide a comprehensive and well-rounded education that promotes academic excellence, critical thinking, and social responsibility. We aim to nurture future leaders who are committed to contributing positively to society and the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution that empowers students to reach their full potential and become globally competitive individuals who embody the values of integrity, compassion, and service.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive to achieve the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and responsibility in our actions.
  • Inclusivity: We foster a welcoming and respectful environment for all students.
  • Service: We believe in giving back to our community and society at large.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity, curiosity, and forward-thinking approaches.

Our Academic Strands

We offer a variety of academic strands tailored to the interests and career goals of our students:

  • General Academic Strand (GAS): A flexible program designed for students exploring different fields.
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM): For students passionate about science and innovation.
  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS): Focused on human behavior, culture, and society.

Why Choose Us?

At Upper Bicutan National High School, we provide:

  • Dedicated and experienced teachers
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
  • A supportive and inclusive community
  • Opportunities for leadership and personal growth

Our Community

We are proud of the vibrant and diverse community we have created at UBNHS. Our students, teachers, and staff come from various backgrounds, and together we celebrate the uniqueness of each individual while working towards a common goal: creating a better future.
